# Kitchen Inventory

A structured inventory of what's in the kitchen, maintained as markdown for use with LLMs.

## Purpose

Provide an LLM with an accurate, up-to-date snapshot of available ingredients, tools, and appliances so it can give relevant recipe suggestions and cooking advice without recommending things we don't have.

## How to Use

Reference `kitchen-inventory.md` for a full list of spices, sauces, always-on-hand pantry items, and cooking tools. In addition to what's listed there, assume standard kitchen equipment is available: non-stick fry pans, knives of various sorts, mixing bowls, measuring spoons and cups, etc.

## About Me

- **Located in Canada** — ingredient suggestions should be available at Canadian grocery stores, Costco, or Amazon.ca
- **Budget conscious** — prefer cost-effective options

## Health Goals

Recipes should support the following goals where possible:
- Reaching 15% body fat
- Maximizing health span — staying functional, strong, and pain-free long-term
- Anti-inflammatory focus — I have psoriatic arthritis and take Simponi (a TNF inhibitor biologic)

## Contents of kitchen-inventory.md

- **Spices** — Full spice rack inventory
- **Always On Hand** — Pantry staples, fridge basics, and fresh produce kept stocked
- **Sauces & Condiments** — Bottles and jars
- **Tools & Appliances** — Broken into Pots & Pans, Tools, and Hardware (with brand/model/manual info where applicable)

## Maintenance

Update `kitchen-inventory.md` whenever something is added, removed, or replaced. Keep measurements in metric.
