# Kitchen Inventory

A structured inventory of what's in the kitchen, maintained as markdown for easy use with LLMs.

## Purpose

Provide an LLM with an accurate, up-to-date snapshot of available ingredients, tools, and appliances so it can give relevant meal planning, recipe suggestions, and cooking advice without recommending things we don't have.

## Contents

- **Spices** — Full spice rack inventory
- **Always On Hand** — Pantry staples, fridge basics, and fresh produce kept stocked
- **Sauces & Condiments** — Bottles and jars
- **Tools & Appliances** — Broken into Pots & Pans, Tools, and Hardware (with brand/model/manual info where applicable)

## Maintenance

Update this file whenever something is added, removed, or replaced. Keep measurements in metric.
