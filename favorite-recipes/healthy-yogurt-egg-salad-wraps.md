# Healthy Yogurt Egg Salad for Wraps (4 Eggs)

📊 Protein: 28g | Fat: 14g | Carbs: 8g | Calories: 280 | Protein Rating: 10/10
*(Full recipe yield for 3–4 wraps)*

> **Macro notes:** Based on 4 large eggs (~24g protein, 20g fat, 2g carbs, 280 cal), ¼ cup plain yogurt (~4g protein, 0–2g fat, 4–6g carbs, 30–50 cal), 1 tsp Dijon mustard (~0g macros), small red onion/garlic/herbs (~2–4g carbs total). No mayo — yogurt keeps it lighter and more anti-inflammatory. Midpoint values used where ranges apply; actuals vary slightly by yogurt brand and egg size.

## Ingredients

- 4 large eggs
- ¼–⅓ cup plain yogurt (or kefir for a tangier result — start with ¼ cup)
- 1–1½ tsp Dijon mustard (or yellow mustard)
- ¼ small red onion, finely diced
- 1 small garlic clove, minced (or ¼–½ tsp granulated garlic)
- ½ tsp dried dill weed (or sub parsley, oregano, or basil)
- ½ tsp smoked paprika
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

**Optional boosts:**
- ¼ tsp ground turmeric (anti-inflammatory)
- Pinch of ground cumin or curry powder

**For serving:**
- Wraps of choice
- Optional extras: sliced tomatoes, olives, or anything crunchy

## Method

1. **Hard-boil the eggs:** Place eggs in a pot, cover with cold water, and bring to a boil. Once boiling, cover the pot, remove from heat, and let sit 9–11 minutes. Transfer to an ice bath to cool, then peel and roughly chop.
2. **Make the dressing:** In a bowl, combine yogurt, mustard, garlic, dill, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper (plus turmeric and/or cumin if using). Stir until smooth.
3. **Combine:** Fold in chopped eggs and diced red onion. Mash gently to your preferred creamy-chunky texture.
4. **Assemble:** Spoon ~⅓–½ cup of filling into each wrap, add any extras, and roll tightly.
5. Enjoy immediately, or chill briefly — flavours meld nicely after 15–20 minutes in the fridge.
