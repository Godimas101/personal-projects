# Tuna Salad Wraps

📊 Protein: 23g | Fat: 15g | Carbs: 28g | Calories: 342 | Protein Rating: 6/10
*(Per wrap, ~5 wraps, includes tortilla)*

Flexible tuna salad that works with whatever flavour direction you're feeling. Makes enough for 4–6 wraps depending on how full you stuff them.

## Core Ingredients (serves 4–6 wraps, using 2 cans tuna)

- 2 cans tuna, well-drained
- 80–120ml mayonnaise (adjust for desired creaminess)
- ¼–½ red onion, finely diced
- 15–30ml Dijon or yellow mustard (Dijon for sophistication, yellow for classic)
- 1–2 garlic cloves, minced or grated (or sub granulated garlic for milder flavour)
- Salt (pink Himalayan or sea salt) and black pepper to taste
- **Optional:** spoonful of yogurt or kefir mixed in with the mayo for a lighter, tangier result

## Flavour Twist — Pick 1–2

| Style | Additions |
|-------|-----------|
| Spicy | 5–10ml sriracha, Frank's Red Hot, Tabasco, cayenne, or crushed red pepper |
| Smoky/earthy | 2.5–5ml smoked paprika + pinch of cumin or onion powder |
| Herby | 2.5–5ml dill weed, parsley, oregano, or basil — dill especially shines in tuna salad |
| Sweet-tangy | 2.5–5ml honey or maple syrup + extra mustard |
| Umami | 2.5–5ml soy sauce or Worcestershire sauce |

## Method

1. Drain tuna well and press out liquid to avoid a soggy salad.
2. Flake tuna in a bowl with a fork.
3. Add mayo, mustard, red onion, garlic, salt, pepper, and your chosen flavour twist.
4. Mix gently until combined. Taste and adjust — more mayo for creamier, more acid or spice for punch.
5. Optional: rest in the fridge 5–10 minutes for flavours to meld.
6. Spread generously on wraps. Add sliced tomatoes or chopped olives for extra texture and brininess.
7. Roll tightly.
