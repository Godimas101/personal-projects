# Weekend Breakfast Shake

📊 Protein: ~48g | Fat: ~22g | Carbs: ~45g | Calories: ~575 | Protein Rating: 7/10
*(Per serving; dual-phase protein from egg whites + milk powder + collagen + gelatin)*

> **Macro notes:** 1 cup whole milk ≈ 8g protein, 8g fat, 12g carbs, 150 cal. ⅓ cup nonfat dry milk powder ≈ 12g protein, 0g fat, 18g carbs, 120 cal. ¼ cup liquid egg whites ≈ 7g protein, 0g fat, 0g carbs, 30 cal. 1 banana ≈ 1g protein, 0g fat, 27g carbs, 105 cal. 15g gelatin ≈ 13g protein, 0g fat, 0g carbs, 50 cal. Collagen 10g ≈ 9g protein. Olive oil 1 tbsp ≈ 14g fat, 120 cal. Greens + other powders contribute minimally to macros. Midpoint values used throughout.

An experiment combining the proven longevity stack from the daily morning shake with old-school 1960s bodybuilder nutrition. Keeps the core supplements (NMN, resveratrol, TMG, creatine, greens, collagen) and layers on dual-phase protein, extra collagen from gelatin, choline from egg, and mineral density from blackstrap molasses.

## Ingredients

### Base (current morning shake minus Vega protein)

- 8.9g (1 scoop) Webber Naturals All Greens Superfoods
- 1.5g BulkSupplements Betaine Anhydrous (TMG)
- 10g (2 scoops) Organika Enhanced Collagen
- 1g BulkSupplements NMN
- 1g BulkSupplements Resveratrol
- 10g (2 scoops) BulkSupplements Creatine Monohydrate
- 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

### Hercules Cocktail Layer

- 1 cup (240 ml) whole milk (or 2% milk)
- ⅓ cup (~40g) nonfat dry milk powder (slow-release casein)
- ¼ cup (60 ml) pasteurized liquid egg whites or 1 whole large egg (fast leucine + choline)
- 1 ripe spotted banana (natural vitamin C for collagen synthesis)
- 10–15g unflavored gelatin (dissolved in a splash of cold water first)
- 1 tbsp blackstrap molasses (mineral boost — iron, calcium, magnesium, potassium)

## Method

1. **Dissolve** gelatin in a little cold water first — let it bloom for 1–2 minutes.
2. **Add** milk, banana, molasses, and egg whites to the blender.
3. **Add** all powders/scoops and olive oil.
4. **Blend** until smooth (30–45 seconds).

## Serve

Drink within 30–60 minutes of training or as your main morning meal. Still finish all calories by 6–7 PM to protect your overnight fast.

> **Note:** This replaces the Vega Organic Protein & Greens scoop from the weekday shake. The milk powder + egg whites + gelatin provide a higher total protein count with dual-phase absorption (fast from egg, slow from casein). Cost per serving is still under $2.50 CAD using Costco/grocery staples.
