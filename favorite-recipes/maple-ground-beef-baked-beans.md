# Maple Ground Beef Baked Beans

Hearty, saucy baked beans with ground beef. Serves 6–8 as a main or side. Total time ~1–2 hours, mostly hands-off.

## Ingredients

**Beans:**
- 200–400ml dried navy beans (rinse and soak)
- Water to cover
- Optional aromatics while cooking: bay leaf, star anise, or cloves

**Meat & Base:**
- 450g ground beef
- 15–30ml coconut oil
- Red onion, diced
- 3–4 garlic cloves, minced

**Sauce:**
- 1 can diced tomatoes
- 30–60ml tomato paste
- 60–120ml maple syrup
- 15–30ml Dijon or yellow mustard
- 15–30ml Worcestershire sauce
- 60–120ml ketchup
- 5ml smoked paprika
- 2.5ml ground cumin
- Onion powder, salt, black pepper to taste
- **Optional heat:** ground cayenne pepper

## Method

1. **Cook beans:** Rinse and soak dried navy beans. Cook in Instant Pot on high pressure 30–40 minutes + natural release (add bay leaf, star anise, or cloves to the water for extra aroma if desired). Or soak overnight and simmer on stove until tender. Drain but reserve some cooking liquid.

2. **Brown the base:** Heat coconut oil in a cast iron pan or using Instant Pot sauté mode. Brown ground beef with diced red onion and minced garlic. Drain excess fat if needed.

3. **Build the sauce:** Stir in canned diced tomatoes, tomato paste, maple syrup, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, ketchup, and all spices.

4. **Combine:** Add cooked beans and a splash of reserved bean liquid for sauciness. Stir to combine.

5. **Simmer:** Cook on stove or Instant Pot sauté for 20–30 minutes to meld flavours.

6. **Bake (optional but recommended):** Transfer to a roasting pan or oven-safe dish. Bake uncovered at 175–200°C for 30–60 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened and bubbly.

7. **Finish:** Taste and adjust — more maple or honey for sweetness, mustard or Worcestershire for tang, smoked paprika for more smoke.
