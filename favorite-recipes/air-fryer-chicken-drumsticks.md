# Air Fryer Chicken Drumsticks with Roasted Veggies

Crispy drumsticks with caramelized veggies, all in the air fryer. Total time ~30–40 minutes.

## Ingredients

**Chicken:**
- Chicken drumsticks, patted dry
- Coconut oil, melted
- Montreal Chicken seasoning (Clubhouse) — or use the spice mix below
- Extra smoked paprika and granulated garlic

**Veggies:**
- Sweet peppers, sliced into strips or chunks
- White mushrooms, halved or sliced
- Green string beans, ends trimmed
- 15–30ml coconut oil, melted

**Spice Mix (for veggies — and chicken if not using Montreal Chicken):**
- Granulated garlic (or minced garlic cloves)
- 2.5–5ml smoked paprika
- 2.5–5ml ground cumin
- 2.5–5ml turmeric
- 2.5–5ml curry powder or garam masala
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- **Optional heat:** ground cayenne or crushed red pepper
- **Optional finish:** splash of soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, or balsamic vinegar over the veggies

## Method

1. **Prep veggies:** Toss sweet peppers, mushrooms, and green beans in a bowl with melted coconut oil and the full spice mix. Set aside.
2. **Prep chicken:** Pat drumsticks dry. Rub with a little coconut oil, then season generously with Montreal Chicken seasoning (or the spice mix + extra smoked paprika and granulated garlic).
3. **Air fry chicken:** Preheat air fryer to 200°C. Place drumsticks in a single layer. Air fry at 200°C for 15 minutes, flip, then cook another 8–12 minutes until internal temp hits 74°C and skin is crispy.
4. **Air fry veggies:** In the last 10–12 minutes of the chicken, add the veggie mix in a single layer. Shake halfway through. Cook until beans are tender-crisp, peppers are softened with char, and mushrooms are golden (total veggie time ~10–15 min).
5. **Serve:** Plate drumsticks with roasted veggies. Splash soy sauce or balsamic vinegar over the veggies for extra tang if desired.
