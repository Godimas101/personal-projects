# Saturday Pancakes

📊 Protein: 24g | Fat: 4g | Carbs: 106g | Calories: 528 | Protein Rating: 4/10
*(Per serving; net carbs ~97g after ~9g fibre)*

> **Macro notes:** High-protein mix keeps protein respectable for a pancake breakfast, but the maple syrup is the calorie driver — adding 216 cal and ~56g carbs on its own. A treat meal, not a macro-optimized one.

Saturday morning staple. One serving of the Flourish mix, blueberries, and maple syrup.

## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup [Flourish Buttermilk Protein Pancake & Waffle Mix](https://www.costco.ca/p/-/flourish-buttermilk-protein-pancake-waffle-mix-1-kg/100563997) (1 serving)
- ½ cup blueberries
- ¼ cup maple syrup

## Method

Follow the instructions on the Flourish package for the pancake/waffle preparation.

## Serve

Top with blueberries and maple syrup.
