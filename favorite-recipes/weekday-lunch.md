# Weekday Lunch

📊 Protein: 55g | Fat: 35g | Carbs: 45g | Calories: 670 | Protein Rating: 8/10
*(Full lunch: 3 egg cups + bean salad serving + frozen veg; bean salad macros include coconut oil)*

> **Macro notes:** Egg cups are the primary protein driver — whole eggs plus egg whites keep fat moderate while maximising protein. The spiced bean salad adds plant-based protein and complex carbs. Normandy veg adds fibre and micronutrients with negligible calories. A strong, balanced weekday lunch.

A high-protein, meal-prepped weekday lunch. Batch-cooked for the week — everything reheats in under 5 minutes.

## Components

### Egg Cups *(recipe for 1 — make 3)*

- 1 egg
- ¼ cup egg whites
- ¼ cup chopped spinach
- 1 cherry tomato
- ½ tsp ground turmeric
- ½ tsp ground ginger
- ½ tsp garlic powder

### Bean Salad

- ¾ cup [Indian Spiced Beans](./indian-spiced-beans.md)

### Frozen Vegetables

- 1 cup Kirkland Normandy-style vegetable blend — broccoli, cauliflower, orange baby carrots, yellow carrots ([Costco](https://sameday.costco.ca/store/costco-canada/products/16297926-kirkland-signature-normandy-style-vegetable-blend-5-5-lb))

## Method

**Egg Cups** (batch for the week):
1. Preheat oven to 175°C. Lightly grease a muffin tin.
2. Divide chopped spinach evenly across 3 cups. Add one cherry tomato to each.
3. Whisk together eggs and egg whites with turmeric, ginger, and garlic powder. Pour evenly over the spinach and tomatoes.
4. Bake 18–22 minutes until set and lightly golden on top.
5. Cool, portion into containers with bean salad and frozen veg, and freeze.

**Frozen Veg:**
Added raw (straight from the bag) when portioning containers — they cook when the meal is microwaved at work.

## Serve

3 egg cups alongside a bean salad serving and the veg. Reheat from frozen in the microwave.
