# Weekday Lunch

📊 Protein: 55g | Fat: 35g | Carbs: 45g | Calories: 670 | Protein Rating: 8/10
*(Full lunch: 3 egg cups + bean salad serving + frozen veg; bean salad macros include coconut oil)*

> **Macro notes:** Egg cups are the primary protein driver — whole eggs plus egg whites keep fat moderate while maximising protein. The spiced bean salad adds plant-based protein and complex carbs with meaningful fat from coconut oil. Normandy veg adds fibre and micronutrients with negligible calories. A strong, balanced weekday lunch.

A high-protein, meal-prepped weekday lunch. Egg cups batch well for the week; the bean salad is made using the [Indian Spiced Beans](./indian-spiced-beans.md) recipe.

## Components

### Egg Cups (makes 3)

| Ingredient | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Eggs | 3 |
| Egg whites | 180ml |
| Chopped spinach | 180ml |
| Cherry tomatoes | 3 |

### Bean Salad

| Ingredient | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| White navy beans | 80ml |
| Chickpeas | 80ml |
| Red kidney beans | 80ml |

*Prepared using the [Indian Spiced Beans](./indian-spiced-beans.md) recipe.*

### Frozen Vegetables

| Ingredient | Amount | Notes | Link |
|-----------|--------|-------|------|
| Normandy-style vegetable blend | 120ml | Mix of broccoli, cauliflower, orange baby carrots, and sliced yellow carrots | [Costco](https://sameday.costco.ca/store/costco-canada/products/16297926-kirkland-signature-normandy-style-vegetable-blend-5-5-lb) |

## Method

**Egg Cups** (batch for the week):
1. Preheat oven to 175°C. Lightly grease a muffin tin.
2. Divide chopped spinach evenly across 3 cups. Add one cherry tomato to each.
3. Whisk together eggs and egg whites. Pour evenly over the spinach and tomatoes.
4. Bake 18–22 minutes until set and lightly golden on top.
5. Cool and refrigerate — reheat in the microwave as needed throughout the week.

**Frozen Veg:**
Microwave 120ml Normandy blend according to package directions, or steam briefly on the stove.

## Serve

3 egg cups alongside a serving of spiced beans and the steamed veg. Everything can be reheated from the fridge in under 5 minutes.
