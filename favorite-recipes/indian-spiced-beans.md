# Simple Indian-Style Spiced Beans

Warm, aromatic curry-style beans. Easy weeknight meal — great over rice or with flatbread.

## Ingredients

- Cooked bean mix (navy beans, chickpeas, kidney beans)
- 15–30ml coconut oil
- Red onion, diced
- 3–4 garlic cloves, minced
- 5–10ml curry powder
- 5ml ground cumin
- 2.5–5ml ground turmeric
- 2.5ml garam masala
- 2.5ml smoked paprika
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- **Optional heat:** ground cayenne pepper or crushed red pepper
- **Optional finish:** splash of almond milk, kefir, or yogurt for creaminess — or a drizzle of honey for subtle sweetness

## Method

1. Heat coconut oil in a pan or pot over medium heat.
2. Sauté diced red onion until soft, 5–7 minutes. Add minced garlic and cook 1 minute more.
3. Stir in all spices (curry powder, cumin, turmeric, garam masala, smoked paprika, salt, pepper). Toast 30–60 seconds until fragrant.
4. Add cooked/drained beans and 120–240ml water or bean cooking liquid to reach a saucy consistency.
5. Simmer 10–15 minutes to let flavors meld.
6. Stir in almond milk, kefir, or yogurt at the end if desired. Taste and adjust seasoning.

## Serve

Hot as a main over rice, or as a side. Pairs well with flatbread.
