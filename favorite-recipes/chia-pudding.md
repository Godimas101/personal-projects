# Weekday Chia Pudding

📊 Protein: ~35g | Fat: ~33g | Carbs: ~39g | Calories: ~600 | Protein Rating: 6/10
*(Per serving; net carbs ~25g after ~14g fibre)*

> **Macro notes:** Chia seeds are the bulk of the calories and fat — rich in omega-3 ALA and soluble fibre. Hemp hearts and flax add additional protein and healthy fats. Walnuts contribute significant fat and calories but bring polyphenols and ALA. Blueberries are the primary carb source. The protein scoop brings protein up meaningfully and adds a second greens hit on top of the morning shake.

Prep the night before — just mix and refrigerate. Ready in the morning with zero effort.

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 tbsp hemp hearts
- 1 tbsp ground flax seeds
- 2 tbsp walnut halves
- ½ cup frozen blueberries
- 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
- 38g (1 scoop) Vega Organic Protein & Greens — vanilla

## Method

1. Combine chia seeds, hemp hearts, ground flax, and protein powder in a jar or bowl.
2. Pour in almond milk and stir well — mix thoroughly to avoid protein powder clumping.
3. Let sit 5 minutes, stir again to break up any clumps.
4. Cover and refrigerate overnight (minimum 4 hours).

## Serve

Cold, straight from the fridge. Top with walnut halves and frozen blueberries — the blueberries will thaw by the time you eat.
