# Weekday Chia Pudding

📊 Protein: 41g | Fat: 50g | Carbs: 50g | Calories: 805 | Protein Rating: 6/10
*(Per serving; net carbs ~28g after ~22g fibre)*

> **Macro notes:** Chia seeds (60ml/~45g) are the bulk of the calories and fat — rich in omega-3 ALA and soluble fibre. Hemp hearts and flax add additional protein and healthy fats. Walnuts contribute significant fat and calories but bring polyphenols and ALA. Blueberries are the primary carb source. The Vega protein scoop brings the protein up meaningfully and adds a second greens hit on top of the morning shake.

Prep the night before — just mix and refrigerate. Ready in the morning with zero effort.

## Ingredients

* 60ml chia seeds
* 15ml hemp hearts
* 15ml ground flax seeds
* 60ml walnut halves
* 120ml frozen blueberries
* 360ml unsweetened almond milk
* 38g (1 scoop) Vega Organic Protein & Greens — vanilla

## Method

1. Combine chia seeds, hemp hearts, ground flax, and protein powder in a jar or bowl.
2. Pour in almond milk and stir well to combine — mix thoroughly to avoid protein powder clumping.
3. Let sit 5 minutes, stir again to break up any clumps.
4. Cover and refrigerate overnight (minimum 4 hours).

## Serve

Cold, straight from the fridge. Top with walnut halves and frozen blueberries — the blueberries will thaw by the time you eat.
